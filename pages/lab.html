Four Bodily Humors

Humans are born with an amount of black and white substance.

Humans can then be fundamentally divided into four bodily humors. These were called sanguine, choleric, melancholic and phlegmatic. A positive bright person is sanguine. A negative bright person is melancholic. A positive strong person is phlegmatic. A negative strong person is choleric. The three doshas are similar, but the sanguine and melancholic are merged into Vata.
